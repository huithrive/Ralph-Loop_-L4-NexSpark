# Yamabushi x Auxora Agency Operations Spec
## 代理商执行运营规范（客户侧简化交互映射）

Version: 1.0

---

## 1) Objective | 目标

Define agency-grade operational detail while keeping client interaction simple and guided.

在代理商侧保留完整执行颗粒度，在客户侧提供可跟随的极简流程。

---

## 2) Operating Roles | 运营角色

| Role | Core Responsibility | Key Outputs |
|---|---|---|
| Strategist | GTM assumptions and growth strategy | GTM Plan, KPI framework |
| Media Buyer | Channel execution and optimization | Campaign actions, budget pacing |
| Creative Lead | Creative test roadmap and assets | Creative plan, iteration results |
| Analyst | Weekly reporting and insights | Weekly sync report |
| Account Manager | Client communication and alignment | Meeting notes, approvals |
| PM Observer | Storyboard and UX quality | UX findings and improvement list |

---

## 3) Mandatory Artifacts | 必备交付物

### PreSales
- GTM report (market + audience + channels + 6-month target)
- Consensus checklist

### Contract
- Signed agreement metadata
- Scope boundary matrix

### Execution
- Budget phase sheet
- Channel plan
- Weekly sync report
- Risk register
- Decision log

### Optimization
- OpenClaw alerts summary
- Applied action log
- Monthly strategy adjustment memo

---

## 4) Weekly Meeting SOP | 周会标准流程

## Before Meeting
1. Pull latest KPI data
2. Update weekly draft
3. Flag blockers and risks

## In Meeting
1. KPI delta review (vs target and last week)
2. Channel deep-dive (Meta/Google)
3. Audience and creative findings
4. Action plan and owner assignment
5. Client Q&A and approvals

## After Meeting
1. Publish final weekly sync
2. Create task tickets
3. Update risk register and decision log

---

## 5) Client-Facing Simplification | 客户侧简化策略

### Client View Should Show
- Current stage
- This week progress
- Top 3 KPIs
- Next 3 actions
- Risks requiring client input

### Client View Should Hide by Default
- Low-level ad set parameters
- Technical attribution details
- Internal ops/debug logs

---

## 6) Deliverable Quality Checklist | 交付质量清单

Each weekly sync must include:
- Clear executive summary
- KPI table with variance explanation
- What worked / what did not
- Specific next actions with owner and due date
- Open risks and mitigation

Each monthly review must include:
- Trend analysis
- Budget efficiency
- Strategic recommendation
- Changes from prior month

---

## 7) Risk & Escalation SOP | 风险升级SOP

Risk levels:
- Low: monitor
- Medium: add mitigation task
- High: discuss in weekly sync and assign owner
- Critical: same-day escalation to agency admin and client stakeholder

Escalation package:
- issue description
- business impact
- attempted fixes
- proposed options
- recommended decision

---

## 8) Acceptance Criteria | 验收标准

1. Agency team has clear weekly and monthly execution rhythm.
2. Client can understand progress and next steps without ad-tech expertise.
3. Every deliverable has owner, status, and due date.
4. Risks and decisions are traceable end-to-end.
