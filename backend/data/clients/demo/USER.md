# Client Profile: YamaBushi Farms

## Brand Overview
**Brand Name:** YamaBushi Wagyu  
**Client Contact:** Kenji Tanaka  
**Industry:** Premium D2C Food & Beverage (Wagyu Beef)  
**Website:** yamabushiwagyu.com  
**Launch Date:** January 2026

## Business Model
- Direct-to-consumer premium Japanese Wagyu beef
- Subscription boxes (monthly) + one-time purchases
- Price range: $89-$299 per box
- Target AOV: $150
- Target ROAS: 3.0+
- Max CPA: $45

## Target Audience
### Primary
- Age: 35-55
- Income: $100k+
- Food enthusiasts, home chefs, grill masters
- Values quality, authenticity, provenance

### Secondary
- Corporate gifting (holidays, client appreciation)
- Special occasions (anniversaries, celebrations)

## Geographic Focus
- Primary: California, Texas, New York, Florida
- Expansion planned: Pacific Northwest, Colorado

## Brand Voice & Preferences
- **Tone:** Premium but approachable, educational not pretentious
- **Messaging:** Focus on Japanese heritage, sustainable farming, butchery craftsmanship
- **Visual Style:** Clean, modern, high-quality food photography
- **Communication Preference:** WhatsApp for urgent alerts, email for reports

## Goals & KPIs
- **Month 1-3:** Build brand awareness, acquire first 500 customers
- **Month 4-6:** Scale to $100k/month revenue
- **Month 7-12:** Achieve profitability, 30% repeat purchase rate

## Current Setup
- Shopify store: Live but not optimized
- Meta Pixel: Installed
- Google Analytics: Configured
- Email: Klaviyo (basic flows only)
- No active ad campaigns yet

## Concerns & Risk Tolerance
- Budget: $15k/month for ads (will scale with ROAS)

- Nervous about ad fraud and wasted spend
- High expectations for data transparency
